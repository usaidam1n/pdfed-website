FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?

Yes! The core features including editing text, signing, and basic annotations are completely free to use forever. Advanced features may be added as premium options in the future.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We operate with a privacy-first architecture. Your files are processed locally within your browser and are never uploaded to our servers. Your documents never leave your device.

Does it work on all websites?

pdfed works on any PDF file you open in Chrome or Firefox, whether it's from a website URL or a local file from your computer.

What browsers are supported?

pdfed currently works on Chrome, Edge, Brave, and other Chromium-based browsers. Firefox support is coming soon!
