Privacy Policy

We believe your data belongs to you. That's why we built pdfed to process everything locally on your device.

1. Local Processing Architecture

pdfed is different. Unlike other online PDF editors that require you to upload your sensitive documents to their servers, pdfed operates entirely within your web browser.

This means:

  • Your files never leave your device.
  • We cannot see, access, or store your documents.
  • All edits, signatures, and redactions happen locally using WebAssembly and JavaScript technologies.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect personal usage data, document contents, or metadata. We are a privacy-first tool designed for security-conscious users.

We do NOT use:

  • Google Analytics or third-party tracking cookies.
  • Server-side logging of user activity.
  • Ad retargeting pixels.

3. Permissions

The extension requires certain permissions to function, which are strictly used for the intended features:

  • "Read and change all your data on the websites you visit" (Host Permissions): This generic warning appears because pdfed needs to inject the toolbar into the PDF viewer tab. We only activate on PDF files.
  • "Storage": Used to save your local preferences (like favorite stamps or signatures) locally in your browser sync storage.

4. Third-Party Services

pdfed is a standalone extension. We do not transmit data to third-party APIs for OCR or conversion. All features are built-in and run locally.

5. Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify users through the extension update notes.
